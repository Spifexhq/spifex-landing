## Overview

This cookie notice ("Cookie Notice") explains how Spifex ("we", "our", "us") uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when visiting our website at www.spifex.com and when using Spifex's services (together, the "Services"). This notice outlines what these technologies are, why we use them, and your rights to control our use of them. If you have any questions, feel free to contact us at privacy@spifex.com.

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. Cookies set by the website owner (in this case, Spifex) are called "First Party Cookies". Only the website owner can access these First Party Cookies. Cookies set by parties other than the website owner are called "Third Party Cookies". These third-party cookies enable additional features or functionality, such as advertising, interactive content, and social sharing. These third parties can recognize your device both when it visits the website in question and when it visits other websites that partner with them. 

Spifex uses cookies and similar technologies like pixel tags, web beacons, clear GIFs, mobile identifiers, and JavaScript (collectively "Cookies") to enable our servers to recognize your web browser and tell us how and when you visit and use our Services.

## How We Use Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use Cookies for the following purposes:

### 1) Essential Cookies
These cookies are necessary for the basic functioning of the Services. They allow you to log into secure areas and ensure the security of the Services.

### 2) Functional Cookies
Functional cookies help remember your preferences, choices, and settings when you visit the Services, ensuring a more personalized experience.

### 3) Performance/Analytical Cookies
These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with the Services by collecting information such as the number of visitors, which pages are viewed, and how long they stay. They also assist us in measuring the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns.

### 4) Retargeting/Advertising Cookies
These cookies track your online activity and preferences so we can present you with relevant web and email content. They help us score leads and provide more targeted advertisements.

We may also use cookies to collect personal information, or combine collected information with other data, in accordance with our Privacy Policy.

## How to Control Cookies and Other Technologies

### 1) Cookie Preferences Center
You can manage your cookie preferences through the “Cookie Preferences” module available at the bottom of the Spifex website.

### 2) Browser Controls
You can also control cookies via your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to disable cookies, block them, or receive alerts when new cookies are set. Please refer to your browser’s help section for more information on how to disable cookies.

### 3) Do Not Track
Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" (DNT) feature, which signals websites not to track your online activity. Currently, there is no industry consensus on how to interpret DNT signals, so our Services, like many others, do not support DNT requests.

### 4) Third-Party Cookies
In addition to managing cookies through your browser, you can opt out of certain third-party cookies. For example, we use Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our Services. You can opt out of Google Analytics cookies by installing the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on. 

For more information about opting out of third-party cookies or targeted advertising, visit [Network Advertising Initiative](http://www.aboutads.info/choices/) or [Your Online Choices](http://www.youronlinechoices.com).
